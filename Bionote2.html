Darwin P. Tiqui

"The Kid"

My Page

Darwin Pernia Tiqui (born December 29,1999) is a college student at the University of the East - Manila, where he is currently taking Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE), His interest in Technology began during his Junior Highschool where he learned some basic video editing. After watching his father working his IT skills for years, he knew at a very young age that he also wanted to go into the technology. As years passed by his curiosity in technology grows he learns some basic programming skills and languages.

"Find that thing you are super passionate about" - Mark Zuckerberg

When he was in High school, darwin participated in some extra curricular activities. He Joined the Altar Server Ministry at his former School Holy Trinity Academy. He became the Team Co-Captain of the Aces Basketball Club and he also graduated with High Honors in his senior High school year.

Besides technology, he has a very big interest in sports, especially Basketball, Darwin is a player of the CpE Warriors during the CpE Basketball League (CpEBL) He plays as the shooting guard and the small forward positions. He became part of the CpE Basketball League 2nd Mythical Team during his sophomore year. He also joined several basketball League in different areas in Metro Manila.
